Reason to use?

  1. Piped is open source.
  2. Each file is stored on discord in 25 mb chunks with unique encryption keys and iv.
  3. Piped never stores or logs the decryption key for your files. If you lose your download link you will no longer be able to access the file. Files can't be accessed by us or other third parties.

FAQ.

How long will my files be online?
If a file is not accessed within a year. It will be deleted.
What are the limit of uploads?
You are free to upload as much as you want.
Any restrictions on downloads?
No. We do not enforce any form of bandwidth limitations on downloads.
